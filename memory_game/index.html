Instructions

"Husker Du?" is a Danish board game, often played by children. This translates into English as "Do you remember?" The board is of a circular design with images printed underneath a rotating plastic wheel. Plastic tokens cover the images, challenging the children to remember which paired images are where - hence the name, "Do you remember?" and allows for different variations of image locations based on rotations per each round.

For this version, simply match the cards below by flipping them over.
